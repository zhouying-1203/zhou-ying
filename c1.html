Eloheh (pronounced el-oh-HAY) takes its name from a Cherokee word meaning "harmony" or "earth," reflecting our commitment to balance‚Äîbetween nature and science, prevention and treatment, people and purpose.

What We Do

  • Virtual and in-person health consultations
