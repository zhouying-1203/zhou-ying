Our Story

At Eloheh, we believe that access to compassionate, high-quality healthcare products and services is a right—not a privilege. Founded on the principle of holistic wellness and care for the whole person, Eloheh has grown from a small family-owned clinic into a trusted provider of innovative healthcare solutions for individuals and families across the region.

Eloheh (pronounced el-oh-HAY) takes its name from a Cherokee word meaning "harmony" or "earth," reflecting our commitment to balance—between nature and science, prevention and treatment, people and purpose.

Our journey began in 2012 when our founder, Dr. Lila Thompson, a practicing physician and public health advocate, recognized a growing need for integrative healthcare services that bridged clinical expertise with everyday wellness. What started as a single clinic offering preventive care and health counseling soon expanded into a comprehensive healthcare brand offering:

What We Do

  • Medical and wellness products (from supplements to mobility aids)
  • Virtual and in-person health consultations
  • Chronic disease management and wellness coaching
  • Community health outreach programs
  • Customized health plans for families and small businesses

Whether you're managing a chronic condition, seeking preventive care, or simply striving for a healthier lifestyle, Eloheh is here to support every step of your journey.
